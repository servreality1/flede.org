Mixed reality appears as a fusion of the real world and the virtual world, so it is considered the next evolution of virtual reality. Tom Ference, founder of the National Center for Supercomputer Applications, first coined the term mixed reality. In a 1993 article, he described a new paradigm of human-computer interaction.

Mixed reality allows you to interact with objects in a virtual environment as if they were actually there. This kind of modern technology can be considered the next big thing for customers and companies, as mixed reality allows digital technology to merge with physical space. Mixed reality capabilities involve creating immersive experiences that allow users to interact with the digital world in new ways. This allows not only to move through virtual environments, but also to manipulate three-dimensional objects and interact with holographic objects. The availability of a huge number of mixed reality development offerings for many companies provides an opportunity to take advantage of these innovations to create unique experiences to engage their customers and help differentiate themselves in the marketplace from their competitors.

What types of mixed reality are there?

Augmented reality can be considered a type of mixed reality, with which it is possible to superimpose digital information on the real world. For example, it is possible to use this technology when choosing the right interior and furniture for the house, before making repairs, buying or making furniture.

A kind of mixed reality is also a virtual reality, which allows you to dive into a completely different world. In this case, there is an opportunity to experience an incredible experience without leaving home, just enough to use the appropriate headset.

What are the advantages of mixed reality development service?

Mixed reality technology is designed to change the way we live and work today. With its help there is an opportunity to completely change many industries such as construction, manufacturing, healthcare and others. Also, mixed reality for development services can be used in many different ways.
